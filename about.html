MY DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

In designing experiences, I put the user at the center of it all. The user- the customer- is the protagonist of a corporation's story. At least that's how I like to see it. I see a UX designer's role as mediatory- we act as the advocate of the users to the organization. How does this affect the organization? Well, the users of a company's product are the reason the company can continue to exist! If a user has a positive experience with a company's product, it's quite likely they'll develop a sense of brand loyalty.

I believe in Ockham's Razor. Sometimes, the simplest solution to a problem is the best. I love to try and apply this principle to my design challenges. I would say modern minimalist design trends are derivatives of this too. What's more, simpler design solutions are easy to implement and better for the user- no one wants to go treasure hunting for information they could've gotten in 3 clicks! :)
